## What is D2 Hora Chart? {#what-is-d2-hora-chart}

**D2 Hora Chart** represents a powerful divisional chart within the ancient framework of Vedic astrology that specifically focuses on wealth and financial prosperity. This sub-divisional chart is derived from the main birth chart (D1) and provides deeper insights into an individual's financial potential and wealth accumulation patterns.

The term "Hora" itself means "half a sign" and represents the day and night cycles, measuring 15 degrees within each sign. Each zodiac sign is divided into two halves or horas, resulting in a total of 24 horas across the entire zodiac. Importantly, these horas are ruled by only two signs: Leo (governed by the Sun) and Cancer (governed by the Moon).

The D2 Hora Chart works as a specialized lens to examine:

- **Financial prosperity**: Your capacity to generate and maintain wealth
- **Source of income**: Whether wealth comes through inheritance or self-effort
- **Asset accumulation**: Patterns of saving and investment success
- **Material well-being**: Overall financial stability throughout life

### Historical Context
The concept of D2 Hora Chart originates from classical Vedic texts including the Brihat Samhita and Parasara's teachings, where ancient sages documented the relationship between wealth analysis and planetary positions. Unlike modern misinterpretations showing 12 houses and signs, the authentic D2 chart consists of only two signs: Cancer and Leo, representing maternal and paternal wealth influences respectively.
