## What is D3 Drekkana Chart? {#what-is-title}

**D3 Drekkana Chart** is a divisional chart in Vedic astrology derived by dividing each zodiac sign into three equal parts of 10 degrees each, resulting in three Drekkanas per sign and a total of 36 across the zodiac[3][2]. Its primary role is to offer deep insights into aspects governed by the third house of the birth chart, focusing especially on:

- **Siblings and sibling relationships**
- **Courage and valor**
- Family dynamics, short travels, communication, and immediate actions[4][5]
- Hidden aspects of one’s nature and skillset

### Historical Context

The foundations of the D3 Drekkana Chart are rooted in ancient astrological texts like the Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra and other classics. Sage Parashara outlined the significance of Drekkana for analyzing co-borns, valor, and the karmic fruits of our actions[1][8]. The Drekkana’s application goes beyond siblings, extending into the realms of karma, courage, and even the psychological traits shaped by fraternal bonds.

## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Date, time, and place of birth (for accurate natal and D3 chart casting)
- Degree positions of planets and significant points in your birth chart

**Calculation Formula**:

1. **Divide Each Zodiac Sign**: Each of the 12 signs (30° each) is split into three Drekkanas (0°-10°, 10°-20°, 20°-30°).
2. **Allocate Planetary Positions**: Place each planet from the birth chart into its corresponding Drekkana segment based on degree.
3. **Construct the D3 Chart**: The new chart displays planetary positions by Drekkana, highlighting third-house influences.
4. **Interpret**: Focus on the 3rd house, its lord, and aspects for sibling and courage readings[3][4].

## Frequently Asked Questions {#faqs}

**Q1. What is the main use of the D3 Drekkana Chart?**  
The D3 Drekkana Chart is primarily used to assess relationships with siblings, inner courage, and outcome of efforts, all through a detailed view of the third house in your horoscope[2][4].

**Q2. Can the D3 Chart reveal the number of siblings?**  
Yes. Placement and lordships in the D3 chart, along with gender of planetary lords, help indicate both number and gender of siblings[6][10].

**Q3. How does the D3 Chart influence courage?**  
By examining the 3rd house, Mars, and placement of benefics or malefics in the D3, astrologers assess a person’s bravery, resolve, and ability to overcome obstacles[2][4].

**Q4. Are D3 Chart insights relevant if I have no siblings?**  
Absolutely. In absence of siblings, it reflects cousin, close friends, or people who play sibling roles, as well as the individual’s courage and communication patterns[9].

**Q5. How can I combine D3 Chart findings with Vastu principles?**
