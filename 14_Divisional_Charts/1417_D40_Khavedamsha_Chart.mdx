## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Accurate birth time, date, and location
- D1 (birth chart) planetary positions

**Calculation Formula**:
The D40 chart divides each sign into 40 equal parts, with each part representing 45 minutes (0°45′) of a sign. To calculate the D40 position of a planet:

1. Determine the exact degree and minute of the planet in its sign
2. Multiply this position by 40
3. The resulting value indicates the D40 position in signs from the original sign

For example:
- If Ascendant is at 1°23′ in Aries, it falls in the second Khavedamsa
- Moving to the second house from Aries places the Ascendant in Taurus in the D40 chart
- Similarly, if Sun is at 15°22′ in Virgo, it falls in the twenty-first Khavedamsa
- Counting twenty-one signs from Virgo places the Sun in Gemini in the D40 chart
