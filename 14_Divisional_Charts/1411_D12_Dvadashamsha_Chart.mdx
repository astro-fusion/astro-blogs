**D12 Dvadashamsha Chart** represents a powerful divisional chart within the ancient framework of Vedic astrology and Vastu Shastra. Also known as Dwadasamsha or Suryamsha, this chart divides each zodiac sign into 12 equal parts of 2°30' each, creating a detailed map of ancestral and parental influences in one's life. This powerful principle combines:

<h2 id="calculation">Calculation and Analysis Methods</h2>

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Accurate birth time, date, and location
- Complete birth chart (D1) with precise planetary degrees

**Calculation Formula**:
The D12 chart is calculated by dividing each sign into 12 equal parts of 2°30' each. The formula involves determining which division a planet falls into within its sign, then counting that many signs forward from the original sign to place the planet in the D12 chart[5].

For example:
1. If your Sun is at 21°14' in Pisces
2. Divide the position by 2°30': 21°14' ÷ 2°30' = 8.5 (9th division)
3. Count 9 signs from Pisces: Pisces → Aries → Taurus → Gemini → Cancer → Leo → Virgo → Libra → Scorpio
4. The Sun in your D12 chart is placed in Scorpio
