## What is D6 Shashthamsha Chart? {#what-is-title}

**D6 Shashthamsha Chart** is a specialized divisional chart in Vedic astrology, intricately linked to the sixth house of the natal horoscope. This chart zooms in on health, diseases, and the nature of enemies—both seen and unseen—and is traditionally used to assess how these influences manifest in an individual’s life and environment[6][3]. In Sanskrit, “Shashthamsha” literally means “one-sixth,” with the D6 chart dividing each zodiac sign into six equal parts to offer a granular perspective on core life challenges.

- **Astrological significance**: It details the underlying causes of health problems and adversarial situations that may not be apparent in the main birth chart[3].
- **Vastu applications**: Insights from this chart can be translated into practical remedies and alignments within your home or workspace to mitigate illness and conflict.
- **Spiritual dimensions**: It reflects the karmic legacy related to health and negative interactions, offering pathways for healing and resolution[4].

### Historical Context

The roots of the D6 Shashthamsha Chart trace back to classical Vedic texts such as the *Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra*, where divisional charts were devised as keys for deeper understanding of life’s intricate patterns[1]. While not as commonly referenced as the D1 or D9 charts, the D6 (Shashthamsha) has always held importance for those seeking precise predictions and remedies regarding health and hidden obstacles[3].

## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Accurate birth date, time, and location (even minor errors can skew D6 outcomes).
- Objective: Health, enemies, or related area of inquiry.

**Calculation Formula**:
- Each zodiac sign (30°) is divided into six sections of 5° each, forming the basis for the D6 divisions.
- Identify the position of each planet as per these divisions to build the D6 chart matrix[1][7].

**Analysis Guidelines**:
- Focus on the condition of the sixth house lord and malefic/benefic influence over the 6th, 8th, and 12th houses in the D6 chart[3].
