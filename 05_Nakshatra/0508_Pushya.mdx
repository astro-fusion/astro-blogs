## Summary
Pushya Nakshatra, the eighth nakshatra in [Vedic astrology](../index.mdx), is considered one of the most auspicious nakshatras. <h2 id="core-concept">Core Concept of Pushya Nakshatra</h2>

### Basic Information
- **Position**: 3°20' to 16°40' Cancer
- **Ruling Planet**: **[Saturn (Shani)](../03_Planets/0307_Saturn.mdx)**
- **Deity**: Brihaspati (Jupiter/Guru)
- **Symbol**: Udder of a cow, lotus flower, arrow
- **Animal**: Goat
- **Gender**: Male
- **Guna**: Rajas
- **Element**: Water
- **Caste**: Kshatriya

The name "Pushya" derives from the Sanskrit word meaning "nourisher" or "flower," representing the peak of nurturing energy. This nakshatra combines [Saturn's](../03_Planets/0307_Saturn.mdx) discipline with Brihaspati's wisdom, creating a unique blend of practical spirituality.

### The Four Padas (Quarters) of Pushya Nakshatra

The nurturing energy of Pushya is refined and directed by its four padas, all of which fall within the sign of [Cancer (Karka)](../01_Rasi/0104_Karka.mdx):

#### First Pada (3°20' - 6°40' [Cancer](../01_Rasi/0104_Karka.mdx))
- **Navamsa:** [Leo (Simha)](../01_Rasi/0105_Simha.mdx)
- **Ruling Planet:** [Sun (Surya)](../03_Planets/0301_Sun.mdx)
- **Effects:** This pada adds a sense of royal dignity and authority to Pushya’s nurturing nature. Individuals are highly respectful, enjoy high status, and often serve as protective leaders or managers. They have a strong sense of family pride and lineage.
- **Strength:** Authoritative protection and ancestral legacy.

#### Second Pada (6°40' - 10°00' [Cancer](../01_Rasi/0104_Karka.mdx))
- **Navamsa:** [Virgo (Kanya)](../01_Rasi/0106_Kanya.mdx)
- **Ruling Planet:** [Mercury (Budha)](../03_Planets/0304_Mercury.mdx)
- **Effects:** A more practical and analytical expression. These natives are the "detail-oriented" caregivers. They excel in hospitality, healthcare, or any field involving systematic nourishment. They have sharp minds and are very hardworking.
- **Strength:** Systematic care and service-oriented intellect.

#### Third Pada (10°00' - 13°20' [Cancer](../01_Rasi/0104_Karka.mdx))
- **Navamsa:** [Libra (Tula)](../01_Rasi/0107_Tula.mdx)
- **Ruling Planet:** [Venus (Shukra)](../03_Planets/0306_Venus.mdx)
- **Effects:** Focuses on harmony, social grace, and aesthetic nourishment. These individuals are extremely charming, kind-hearted, and artistic. They seek to nourish others through peace, diplomacy, and the creation of beautiful, harmonious environments.
- **Strength:** Diplomacy and artistic nourishment.

#### Fourth Pada (13°20' - 16°40' [Cancer](../01_Rasi/0104_Karka.mdx))
- **Navamsa:** [Scorpio (Vrishchika)](../01_Rasi/0108_Vrishchika.mdx)
- **Ruling Planet:** [Mars (Mangal)](../03_Planets/0303_Mars.mdx)
- **Effects:** The most intense and secretive of the padas. It brings out the deeper, transformative side of nourishment. These natives may be interested in occult healing or transformation through discipline. They are extremely protective and may have intense emotional depths.
- **Strength:** Protective resilience and psychological depth.

<h2 id="mythology">Mythological Background</h2>

According to [Vedic tradition](../index.mdx), Pushya is associated with Brihaspati, the guru of the gods. The mythology tells of how this nakshatra was created to provide divine nourishment and guidance to both gods and humans.

In one legend, when the gods faced challenges in their spiritual development, Brihaspati established Pushya as a celestial center of learning and nourishment. This is why people born under this nakshatra often become natural teachers and guides.

The connection to cow's udder in Hindu mythology represents Kamadhenu, the divine cow that grants all wishes, emphasizing Pushya's ability to fulfill desires through righteous means.
