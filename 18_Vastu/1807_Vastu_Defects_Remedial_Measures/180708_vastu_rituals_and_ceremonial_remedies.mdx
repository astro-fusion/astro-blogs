## Types of Vastu Rituals and Ceremonies {#types-of-vastu-rituals-and-ceremonies}

### Vastu Shanti Puja

- **Principle**: Vastu Shanti Puja is a powerful ceremony that is performed to appease the Vastu Purusha and to remove all Vastu defects from a home.
- **Procedure**: The puja is typically performed by a qualified priest and involves the chanting of mantras, the offering of flowers and fruits, and the performance of a fire ceremony (havan).

### Griha Pravesh Puja

- **Principle**: Griha Pravesh Puja is performed when moving into a new home to bless the space and to protect it from negative energies.
- **Procedure**: The puja is similar to the Vastu Shanti Puja and is performed before the family moves into the new home.

### Navagraha Puja

- **Principle**: Navagraha Puja is performed to appease the nine planets and to mitigate their negative effects.
- **Procedure**: The puja involves the chanting of mantras and the offering of specific items to each of the nine planets.
