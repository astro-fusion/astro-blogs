<h2 id="case-study-1">Case Study 1: From Financial Crisis to Prosperity</h2>

### The Problem

A family was facing a severe financial crisis. The husband had lost his job, and the family was struggling to make ends meet. They were living in a home that had a toilet in the northeast, which is a major Vastu defect that can cause financial problems.

### The Solution

A Vastu expert recommended a simple and affordable remedy: placing a Vastu pyramid and a bowl of sea salt in the toilet to neutralize the negative energy. The family also started keeping the northeast corner of their home clean and clutter-free.

### The Outcome

Within a few months, the husband found a new and better-paying job. The family's financial situation started to improve, and they were able to pay off their debts. Today, they are living a prosperous and happy life.

<h2 id="case-study-2">Case Study 2: Restoring Health and Harmony</h2>

### The Problem

A couple was experiencing constant health problems and arguments. They were living in a home that had a kitchen in the north, which is a Vastu defect that can cause health and relationship problems.

### The Solution

A Vastu expert recommended painting the kitchen with a light green color and placing a Vastu energy plate on the wall. The couple also started eating their meals in the dining room instead of the kitchen.

### The Outcome

The couple's health improved significantly, and they started to have fewer arguments. Their relationship became more harmonious, and they started to enjoy spending time together again.

<h2 id="case-study-3">Case Study 3: Boosting Business Success</h2>

### The Problem

A business was struggling to attract customers and to make a profit. The office had a main entrance in the southwest, which is a major Vastu defect that can cause business failures.

### The Solution

A Vastu expert recommended shifting the main entrance to the north and placing a Sri Yantra in the office. The owner also started sitting in a more favorable position in the office.

### The Outcome

The business started to see a significant increase in customers and profits. The owner was able to expand the business and to hire more employees. Today, the business is a thriving success.
