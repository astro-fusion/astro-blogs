## Case Study 1: From Rags to Riches

- **The Client**: A young entrepreneur who was struggling to make his business profitable.
- **The Problem**: The client's birth chart showed a weak Mercury, the planet of business and communication. His office had a Vastu defect in the North direction (the direction of Mercury).
- **The Solution**: An Astro-Vastu expert recommended a combination of astrological and Vastu remedies. The client was advised to wear an emerald (the gemstone of Mercury) and to chant the Budha mantra. The expert also recommended enhancing the North direction of his office by placing a green plant, a picture of a lush green landscape, and a Budha yantra.
- **The Result**: Within a year, the client's business started to grow exponentially. He is now a successful and wealthy entrepreneur.

## Case Study 2: A Miraculous Recovery

- **The Client**: A middle-aged woman who was suffering from a chronic illness.
- **The Problem**: The client's birth chart showed a weak Sun, the planet of health and vitality. Her bedroom was in the wrong direction, and it had several Vastu defects.
- **The Solution**: An Astro-Vastu expert recommended shifting the client's bedroom to a more favorable direction and correcting the Vastu defects. The client was also advised to wear a ruby (the gemstone of the Sun) and to chant the Surya mantra.
- **The Result**: The client's health improved dramatically. She is now living a healthy and active life.

## Case Study 3: A Tale of Two Businesses

- **The Clients**: Two brothers who owned a business together.
- **The Problem**: The business was not doing well, and the brothers were constantly arguing with each other. Their birth charts showed a weak Jupiter, the planet of wisdom and prosperity.
- **The Solution**: An Astro-Vastu expert recommended enhancing the Northeast direction of their office (the direction of Jupiter) by placing a water feature, a picture of a deity, and a Guru yantra. The brothers were also advised to wear a yellow sapphire (the gemstone of Jupiter).
- **The Result**: The business started to flourish, and the brothers' relationship improved. They are now working together in harmony and are achieving great success.
