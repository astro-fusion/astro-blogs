## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Latitude and longitude of the plot
- Orientation (compass directions)
- Soil analysis report
- Surrounding water sources and topography

**Calculation Formula**:

While traditional Vastu used observation and experience, today’s assessment often includes formulas for:

1. **Plot Proportion:**  
   For residential use, prefer a square (length = breadth) or rectangle (length:breadth up to 2:1).  
   \[
   \text{Aspect Ratio} = \frac{\text{Length}}{\text{Breadth}}
   \]
   Aim for aspect ratio between 1:1 and 2:1 for optimal stability[3][2].

2. **Slope and Drainage:**  
   Calculate natural slope using a surveyor’s level:
   \[
   \text{Slope (\%)} = \frac{\text{Height Difference}}{\text{Horizontal Distance}} \times 100
   \]
   Favor gentle slopes towards the northeast[8].

3. **Soil Quality Index (SQI):**  
   Combine results from pH, organic content, and compaction tests to rate soil fertility and load-bearing capacity.

4. **Solar Orientation:**  
   Chart sunrise and sunset points at different seasons to maximize natural light in key areas[9].
