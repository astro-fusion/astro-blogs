## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Compass orientation (to determine East, West, North, South)
- Plot dimensions and shape
- Slope direction and elevation
- Surrounding features (water bodies, roads, obstructions)

**Calculation Formula**:

1. **Direction Analysis**: Use a compass to identify the plot’s primary facing direction.
2. **Shape Evaluation**: Confirm whether the plot is square, rectangular, or irregular.
3. **Slope and Surroundings**: Note whether the plot slopes toward the North/East (preferred for prosperity) and analyze adjacent features (avoid plots near cemeteries or hospitals)[5][6][7].
4. **Vastu Mapping**: Overlay the Vastu Purusha Mandala (the energy grid) to highlight key zones (health, wealth, relationships).
5. **Remedy Identification**: For any detected flaws (e.g., cut corners, wrong entrance), recommend Vastu remedies like color changes, mirror placement, or architectural corrections[8].

These steps help practitioners and homeowners make data-driven, energetically sound choices.
