## Common Mistakes to Avoid {#common-mistakes}

### Overloading with Plants
While plants are beneficial, too many can create stagnant energy or excessive humidity. Balance is key—select a few quality plants rather than creating an indoor jungle.

### Misplacing Elements
Placing elements in contradictory directions can worsen Vastu defects:
- Avoid water features in fire-dominant areas (southeast)
- Don't place fire elements in water-dominant areas (northeast)
- Refrain from placing thorny plants in the southwest or northeast

### Neglecting Plant Care
Dried, wilting, or dead plants create negative energy. According to Vastu principles, Tulsi plants should be grown with proper care and should never be allowed to dry out or become damaged, as this can bring bad luck[6].

### Ignoring Comprehensive Analysis
Vastu doshas are rarely due to imbalances in a single direction. Multiple directions often contribute to energy problems, requiring a holistic approach to correction[5].
