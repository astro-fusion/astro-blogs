## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Plot orientation and compass directions
- Height differences at each corner of the property

**Calculation Formula**:
1. Measure elevation at each corner (SW, SE, NW, NE).
2. The ideal sequence: Southwest (highest), Southeast/Northwest (medium), Northeast (lowest).
3. Slope direction = Direction of land's lowest point (e.g., slope toward northeast).

**Analysis**:
- Slope toward north or east: Highly auspicious, enhances prosperity and harmony
- Slope toward south or west: Considered inauspicious, may attract challenges and losses[5][7]

## Practical Vastu Applications {#practical-applications}

- **Before purchase**: Use a level to check natural slope; prefer plots with gentle inclines toward north or east[4][5].
- **For existing properties**:  
  - Add soil or construct elevations in the southwest if needed
  - Remove excess soil or avoid depressions in the northeast
  - Redirect water flow so it exits in the north or east, not south or west
- **For sloped buildings (e.g., on hills)**: Roofs and terraces should also slope toward north/east, not south/west[9].
