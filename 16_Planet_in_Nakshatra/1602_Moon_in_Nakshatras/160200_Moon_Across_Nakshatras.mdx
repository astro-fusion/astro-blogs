# Moon Across 27 Nakshatras: Vedic Astrology Insights on Lunar Emotions

## Introduction

In Vedic Astrology, the Moon (Chandra) governs the mind, emotions, and inner experience. As the planet of receptivity and reflection, it shapes how we process feelings, form attachments, and navigate our inner world. Its placement in one of the 27 Nakshatras adds nuanced layers to emotional expression, revealing subtle patterns in personality, behavior, and life tendencies.

Nakshatras are 27 lunar constellations that divide the zodiac into 13°20' segments. The Moon's position in a specific Nakshatra modifies its emotional expression, adding karmic depth, behavioral traits, and unique ways of experiencing the world. This guide explores Moon in all 27 Nakshatras, drawing from classical texts like the Brihat Jataka and modern interpretations.

## Understanding Moon's Strength and Nakshatra Influence

The Moon is exalted in Taurus, debilitated in Scorpio, and strong in Cancer. In Nakshatras, it finds particular strength in Rohini, Hasta, and Shravana (its ruling Nakshatras). A well-placed Moon enhances emotional intelligence and stability, while affliction may lead to mood fluctuations or sensitivity issues.

## Moon in Each Nakshatra

### 1. Ashwini Nakshatra (0°00' – 13°20' Aries)
- **Planetary Lord**: Ketu
- **Deity**: Ashwini Kumaras
- **Symbol**: Horse's Head
- **Gana**: Deva
- **Key Traits**: Quick, alert mind; fond of decoration, pleasant appearance, popular, skilled, intelligent. Responds quickly to challenges, recovers from setbacks with speed. Natural charm and readiness for action.
- **Career Suitability**: Medicine, athletics, emergency services, fields requiring quick thinking.

### 2. Bharani Nakshatra (13°20' – 26°40' Aries)
- **Planetary Lord**: Venus
- **Deity**: Yama
- **Symbol**: Yoni
- **Gana**: Manushya
- **Key Traits**: Determined, truthful, healthy, skillful, generally happy. Serious moral sense, willingness to carry responsibility. Determination supports consistent effort. Health tends to be good, skillfulness across pursuits.
- **Career Suitability**: Creative fields, law, finance, roles involving accountability.

### 3. Krittika Nakshatra (26°40' Aries – 10°00' Taurus)
- **Planetary Lord**: Sun
- **Deity**: Agni
- **Symbol**: Razor or Flame
- **Gana**: Rakshasa
- **Key Traits**: Strong appetite, magnetic personal appearance, tendency toward desire. Sharpness and ability to cut through obstacles. Fame often comes with attention. Intense and driven mind.
- **Career Suitability**: Leadership, administration, public life, creative arts.

### 4. Rohini Nakshatra (10°00' – 23°20' Taurus)
- **Planetary Lord**: Moon
- **Deity**: Brahma
- **Symbol**: Chariot or Ox Cart
- **Gana**: Manushya
- **Key Traits**: Truthful, clean, religious, sweet-spoken, steady mind, handsome appearance. Rooted in values, attracts affection and material comfort naturally. Steady minds, love for beauty and aesthetics.
- **Career Suitability**: Agriculture, arts, music, trade, hospitality.

### 5. Mrigashira Nakshatra (23°20' Taurus – 6°40' Gemini)
- **Planetary Lord**: Mars
- **Deity**: Soma
- **Symbol**: Deer's Head
- **Gana**: Deva
- **Key Traits**: Capricious, skillful, good speakers, hopeful, inclined toward sensory enjoyment. Sensitivity, caution before acting. Communicates well, optimistic. Restlessness drives varied experiences.
- **Career Suitability**: Writing, teaching, travel, research, commerce.

### 6. Ardra Nakshatra (6°40' – 20°00' Gemini)
- **Planetary Lord**: Rahu
- **Deity**: Rudra
- **Symbol**: Teardrop
- **Gana**: Manushya
- **Key Traits**: Self-focused, proud, capable of harsh behavior when unsettled. Sharp perceptive abilities, penetrating intelligence. Turbulent inner life, processes experiences deeply.
- **Career Suitability**: Research, psychology, investigative work, technical fields.

### 7. Punarvasu Nakshatra (20°00' Gemini – 3°20' Cancer)
- **Planetary Lord**: Jupiter
- **Deity**: Aditi
- **Symbol**: Quiver of Arrows
- **Gana**: Deva
- **Key Traits**: Religious, patient, good-natured, content with simple pleasures. Wisdom, generosity, philosophical approach. Capacity to recover and rebuild. Spiritual inclinations.
- **Career Suitability**: Teaching, spiritual work, counseling, healing arts.

### 8. Pushya Nakshatra (3°20' – 16°40' Cancer)
- **Planetary Lord**: Saturn
- **Deity**: Brihaspati
- **Symbol**: Flower, Circle, or Cow's Udder
- **Gana**: Deva
- **Key Traits**: Control over passions, popular, learned, wealthy, charitable. Discipline, commitment to correctness. Generosity earns goodwill. Knowledge used to help others.
- **Career Suitability**: Education, mentorship, religious work, administration, community service.

### 9. Ashlesha Nakshatra (16°40' – 30°00' Cancer)
- **Planetary Lord**: Mercury
- **Deity**: Sarpa (Naga)
- **Symbol**: Coiled Serpent
- **Gana**: Rakshasa
- **Key Traits**: Self-focused, cunning when afflicted. Sharp thinking and verbal sharpness. Observant, perceptive. Skill in strategy, healing, occult understanding.
- **Career Suitability**: Psychology, medicine, intelligence work, research, metaphysics.

### 10. Magha Nakshatra (0°00' – 13°20' Leo)
- **Planetary Lord**: Ketu
- **Deity**: Pitris (Ancestors)
- **Symbol**: Royal Throne
- **Gana**: Rakshasa
- **Key Traits**: Wealthy, deep respect for elders and divine forces, enterprising. Dignity, authority. Reverence for tradition. Enterprises succeed through loyalty.
- **Career Suitability**: Politics, administration, ancestry-related work, ceremonial roles, institutional leadership.

### 11. Purva Phalguni Nakshatra (13°20' – 26°40' Leo)
- **Planetary Lord**: Venus
- **Deity**: Bhaga
- **Symbol**: Front Legs of a Bed or Hammock
- **Gana**: Manushya
- **Key Traits**: Sweet-spoken, generous, handsome, fond of travel, skilled in service. Sociable, warm relationships. Love for arts, music, aesthetic environments.
- **Career Suitability**: Performing arts, music, fashion, hospitality, diplomatic roles.

### 12. Uttara Phalguni Nakshatra (26°40' Leo – 10°00' Virgo)
- **Planetary Lord**: Sun
- **Deity**: Aryaman
- **Symbol**: Rear Legs of a Bed
- **Gana**: Manushya
- **Key Traits**: Popular, happy, self-reliant through own effort. Confidence, purpose, leadership. Contented lives through steady effort. Respected without seeking approval.
- **Career Suitability**: Management, social work, civil services, professions requiring reliability.

### 13. Hasta Nakshatra (10°00' – 23°20' Virgo)
- **Planetary Lord**: Moon
- **Deity**: Savitar
- **Symbol**: Open Hand
- **Gana**: Deva
- **Key Traits**: Enterprising, intelligent, resourceful. Sensitivity, adaptability. Skill in crafts, healing, analytical work. Practical intelligence, resourcefulness.
- **Career Suitability**: Healing, craft, skilled trades, writing, fields requiring dexterity or detail.

### 14. Chitra Nakshatra (23°20' Virgo – 6°40' Libra)
- **Planetary Lord**: Mars
- **Deity**: Tvashtar (Vishwakarma)
- **Symbol**: Bright Jewel
- **Gana**: Rakshasa
- **Key Traits**: Fond of fine clothing, beautiful ornaments, attractive features. Sharpness, competitive drive, excellence. Strong visual and aesthetic sensibility.
- **Career Suitability**: Architecture, interior design, visual arts, fashion, creative fields.

### 15. Swati Nakshatra (6°40' – 20°00' Libra)
- **Planetary Lord**: Rahu
- **Deity**: Vayu
- **Symbol**: Young Sprout
- **Gana**: Deva
- **Key Traits**: Polite, merchant-like, kind-hearted, sweet-spoken, generous. Ambition, independence. Trade, negotiation come naturally. Adaptable, pleasant communication.
- **Career Suitability**: Business, trade, diplomacy, law, negotiations.

### 16. Vishakha Nakshatra (20°00' Libra – 3°20' Scorpio)
- **Planetary Lord**: Jupiter
- **Deity**: Indra-Agni
- **Symbol**: Triumphal Archway
- **Gana**: Rakshasa
- **Key Traits**: Handsome, clever speakers, driven by desire. Knowledgeable, persuasive. Success in debate, leadership. Drive to achieve, potential jealousy.
- **Career Suitability**: Law, politics, public speaking, academia, religious leadership.

### 17. Anuradha Nakshatra (3°20' – 16°40' Scorpio)
- **Planetary Lord**: Saturn
- **Deity**: Mitra
- **Symbol**: Lotus
- **Gana**: Deva
- **Key Traits**: Natural leaders, live away from birthplace, love travel. Discipline, endurance. Strong alliances, leadership with care. Maintain direction in difficulty.
- **Career Suitability**: International work, diplomacy, scientific research, organizational leadership.

### 18. Jyeshtha Nakshatra (16°40' – 30°00' Scorpio)
- **Planetary Lord**: Mercury
- **Deity**: Indra
- **Symbol**: Round Talisman or Umbrella
- **Gana**: Rakshasa
- **Key Traits**: Few intimate friends, contented, charitable, irritable. Mental sharpness, communicative ability. Protective, authoritative. High standards, sensitivity to disorder.
- **Career Suitability**: Leadership roles, administration, protective services, counseling, seniority positions.

### 19. Mula Nakshatra (0°00' – 13°20' Sagittarius)
- **Planetary Lord**: Ketu
- **Deity**: Nirriti
- **Symbol**: Roots of a Plant
- **Gana**: Rakshasa
- **Key Traits**: Proud, wealthy, happy, steady. Spiritual edge, look beneath surface. Stable foundation, enjoyment of pleasures. Drive to root causes.
- **Career Suitability**: Philosophy, research, medicine, spiritual work, investigative fields.

### 20. Purva Ashadha Nakshatra (13°20' – 26°40' Sagittarius)
- **Planetary Lord**: Venus
- **Deity**: Apas (Waters)
- **Symbol**: Elephant's Tusk or Fan
- **Gana**: Manushya
- **Key Traits**: Good spouse, proud nature, steady loyalty. Beauty, aesthetic appreciation. Deep relationships, resilience. Refinement and strength.
- **Career Suitability**: Arts, music, water-related professions, social work, public relations.

### 21. Uttara Ashadha Nakshatra (26°40' Sagittarius – 10°00' Capricorn)
- **Planetary Lord**: Sun
- **Deity**: Vishvadevas
- **Symbol**: Elephant's Tusk or Small Bed
- **Gana**: Manushya
- **Key Traits**: Polite, knowledgeable, virtuous, popular, grateful. Strength of character, moral direction. Virtue and reliability. Gratitude shapes relationships.
- **Career Suitability**: Public service, education, military, administration, leadership roles.

### 22. Shravana Nakshatra (10°00' – 23°20' Capricorn)
- **Planetary Lord**: Moon
- **Deity**: Vishnu
- **Symbol**: Three Footprints or an Ear
- **Gana**: Deva
- **Key Traits**: Wealthy, learned, good-natured, generous. Receptivity, wisdom accumulated over time. Deep listening, intuitive understanding. Respected for character.
- **Career Suitability**: Education, counseling, religious teaching, journalism, knowledge-sharing.

### 23. Dhanishta Nakshatra (23°20' Capricorn – 6°40' Aquarius)
- **Planetary Lord**: Mars
- **Deity**: Eight Vasus
- **Symbol**: Musical Drum
- **Gana**: Rakshasa
- **Key Traits**: Generous, wealthy, courageous, drawn to music and prosperity. Drive, directness, physical energy. Rhythm, vitality. Bold choices for wealth.
- **Career Suitability**: Music, performance, military, sports, real estate, entrepreneurship.

### 24. Shatabhisha Nakshatra (6°40' – 20°00' Aquarius)
- **Planetary Lord**: Rahu
- **Deity**: Varuna
- **Symbol**: Empty Circle or 1000 Stars
- **Gana**: Rakshasa
- **Key Traits**: Truthful, direct, adventurous, overcomes opposition. Intensity, unconventional thinking. Research, healing, hidden knowledge. Directness earns respect.
- **Career Suitability**: Medicine, astrology, research, technology, investigative fields.

### 25. Purva Bhadrapada Nakshatra (20°00' Aquarius – 3°20' Pisces)
- **Planetary Lord**: Jupiter
- **Deity**: Aja Ekapada
- **Symbol**: Front Legs of a Funeral Cot
- **Gana**: Manushya
- **Key Traits**: Skillful, sorrowful at times, careful with resources. Wisdom, philosophical depth. Intense inner world, philosophical inquiry.
- **Career Suitability**: Finance, spiritual practice, occult sciences, research, sustained discipline.

### 26. Uttara Bhadrapada Nakshatra (3°20' – 16°40' Pisces)
- **Planetary Lord**: Saturn
- **Deity**: Ahir Budhnya
- **Symbol**: Rear Legs of a Funeral Cot
- **Gana**: Manushya
- **Key Traits**: Gifted speakers, happy, charitable, succeeds over adversity. Patience, endurance, quiet resilience. Family fulfillment, charitable grounded in concern.
- **Career Suitability**: Spiritual guidance, social work, writing, teaching, public service.

### 27. Revati Nakshatra (16°40' – 30°00' Pisces)
- **Planetary Lord**: Mercury
- **Deity**: Pushan
- **Symbol**: Fish or Drum
- **Gana**: Deva
- **Key Traits**: Well-developed features, popularity, courage, cleanliness, wealth. Intelligence, versatile mind. Movement through experience with ease. Nurturing quality.
- **Career Suitability**: Art, travel, spiritual work, education, creative communication.
