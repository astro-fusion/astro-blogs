# Mercury Across 27 Nakshatras: Vedic Astrology Insights on Intellectual Power

## Introduction

In Vedic Astrology, Mercury (Budha) represents intelligence, logic, communication, speech, learning ability, business, trade, adaptability, humor, wit, and the nervous system. As the planet of the mind, it governs how we think, process information, and express ourselves. Mercury behaves like the Nakshatra lord where it sits, absorbing and adapting the qualities of that lunar mansion.

The placement of Mercury in a specific Nakshatra modifies its expression, revealing unique mental patterns, communication styles, learning approaches, and business instincts. This guide explores Mercury in all 27 Nakshatras, drawing from classical texts and modern interpretations.

## Understanding Mercury's Strength and Nakshatra Influence

Mercury is strong in its own signs (Gemini, Virgo), exalted in Virgo, debilitated in Pisces. In Nakshatras, it shines brightest in Ashlesha, Jyeshtha, and Revati (its ruling Nakshatras). A well-placed Mercury enhances intellectual abilities and communication skills, while affliction may cause nervous disorders or speech impediments.
