## Summary

Ketu, the South Node and planet of liberation in Vedic astrology, represents spirituality, detachment, and karmic completion. Its transit through the 27 Nakshatras brings opportunities for spiritual growth, release of past patterns, and mystical experiences. This comprehensive guide explores Ketu's influence in each lunar mansion, revealing how it facilitates liberation and karmic resolution during its transit period.

---

## Introduction

Ketu, the South Node of the Moon and the headless planet in Vedic astrology, represents the highest spiritual aspirations and ultimate liberation (Moksha). Known as the **dragon's tail**, Ketu embodies detachment from material desires, past life karma, and mystical experiences that lead to enlightenment.

When Ketu transits through different Nakshatras, it activates specific spiritual energies that influence:

- **Spirituality and Liberation**: Ketu dissolves attachments and guides toward enlightenment
- **Past Life Karma**: It brings karmic patterns from previous incarnations for resolution  
- **Detachment and Isolation**: Ketu creates circumstances that foster inner independence
- **Mysticism and Healing**: It opens doors to occult knowledge and spiritual healing
- **Sudden Changes**: Ketu's unpredictable nature brings unexpected transformations

The Nakshatra through which Ketu transits refines these spiritual influences, creating unique combinations of energy that shape the transit's specific effects on consciousness and karmic evolution.

**Key Traits**: Spiritual healing, detached vitality, mystical recovery, karmic health patterns
**Influences**: Alternative medicine, spiritual awakening, past life healing, inner strength development
