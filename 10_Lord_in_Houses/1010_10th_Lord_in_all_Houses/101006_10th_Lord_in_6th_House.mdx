# 10th Lord in 6th House: Professional Success Through Service and Healing

## Summary:
When the 10th Lord occupies the 6th House, it creates professionals who achieve success through service, healing, overcoming challenges, and competitive advantage. This placement indicates career growth through helping others, working in health-related fields, and transforming obstacles into opportunities for advancement.

## The Service-Career Power Connection

The 6th House represents service, health, daily routines, obstacles, competition, and work environment. When the 10th Lord (career significator) resides here, it creates individuals whose professional success comes through dedication to service, overcoming challenges, and often working in fields that heal or help others.

## Understanding 6th House Energies

The 6th House governs:
- **Service and Helping Others**: Careers focused on serving and healing
- **Health and Wellness**: Medical, therapeutic, and wellness professions
- **Daily Work Routines**: Structured, consistent professional practices
- **Obstacles and Challenges**: Overcoming difficulties for growth
- **Competition and Enemies**: Thriving in competitive environments
- **Debt and Litigation**: Legal and financial problem-solving
