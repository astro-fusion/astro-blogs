## Summary:
When the 10th Lord occupies the 6th House, it creates professionals who achieve success through service, healing, overcoming challenges, and competitive advantage. This placement indicates career growth through helping others, working in health-related fields, and transforming obstacles into opportunities for advancement.

## Understanding the 10th Lord in Vedic Astrology

The 10th Lord represents our profession, public standing, and *Karma*. The 6th House represents *Roga* (disease), *Rina* (debt), *Ripu* (enemies), and *Seva* (service).

When the 10th Lord is placed in the 6th House, it connects the career to the house of obstacles and service. This is an *Upachaya* placement (house of growth), indicating that success comes through struggle, hard work, and overcoming competition.

## Classical Interpretation (BPHS)

The *Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra* states:

> **"If the 10th Lord is in the 6th House, the native will be bereft of paternal bliss, troubled by enemies, and will have an unsteady mind."**

### Decoding the Shloka:
1.  **Bereft of Paternal Bliss**: The 10th house is a secondary house for the father. Its lord going to the 6th (a *Dusthana* or evil house) can indicate distance, disagreement, or lack of support from the father.
2.  **Troubled by Enemies**: The career path is not smooth. The native will face competition, jealousy, or office politics. However, the 6th house also gives the power to *defeat* enemies.
3.  **Unsteady Mind**: The constant pressure of work, deadlines, and competition can lead to anxiety or stress.

## 10th Lord in 6th House: Detailed Analysis

### The Service-Oriented Career

-   **Legal & Litigation**: The 6th house rules disputes. This is excellent for lawyers, judges, and arbitrators.
-   **Service Industry**: Any job that involves "serving" or "fixing" problems fits here. This includes IT support, customer service, and consulting.
