## Ruling Planet and Cosmic Alignment

### [Jupiter (Guru): The Lord of Wisdom](../03_Planets/0305_Jupiter.mdx)
As the primary ruler of Dhanu Rashi, Jupiter bestows the qualities of optimism, expansive vision, and ethical righteousness. In Vedic astrology, Jupiter represents our wisdom (Jnana), our luck (Bhagya), and our adherence to spiritual laws (Dharma). Sagittarius is the **Firey home** for Jupiter, where its energy manifests as the search for truth, higher learning, and the ability to guide others toward enlightenment.

### [Jupiter (Guru): Peak Mooltrikona Force](../03_Planets/0305_Jupiter.mdx)
Dhanu is also the **Mooltrikona sign** for Jupiter (specifically from 0° to 10°). This indicates that the planet of expansion acts with its most focused and grounded benevolence here. In Sagittarius, Jupiter is the seeker, the adventurer, and the teacher who applies expansive wisdom to the material world.

## Nakshatra Divisions within Dhanu

Every zodiac sign of 30° is composed of 2.25 Nakshatras. Dhanu Rashi is governed by the following three lunar mansions:

1. **[Mula Nakshatra](../05_Nakshatra/0519_Mula.mdx):** (0° 00' to 13° 20' - *All 4 Padas*) — Ruled by **[Ketu](../03_Planets/0309_Ketu.mdx)**. Known as the "Root," it signifies the probing of core truths and destruction of falsehood, located at the galactic center.
2. **[Purva Ashadha Nakshatra](../05_Nakshatra/0520_Purva_Ashadha.mdx):** (13° 20' to 26° 40' - *All 4 Padas*) — Ruled by **[Venus (Shukra)](../03_Planets/0306_Venus.mdx)**. The "Early Victory" star, it represents the power to invigorate and bring things to fruition through desire and social grace.
3. **[Uttara Ashadha Nakshatra](../05_Nakshatra/0521_Uttara_Ashadha.mdx):** (26° 40' to 30° 00' - *Pada 1 only*) — Ruled by **[Sun (Surya)](../03_Planets/0301_Sun.mdx)**. This initial segment brings solar leadership and the "Later Victory" through spiritual discipline and collective responsibility.

## Personality Traits and Behavioral Analysis

Dhanu natives reflect the expansive nature of Jupiter combined with the Fiery element:

- **Strengths**:
  - **Optimism**: Unwavering belief in the positive outcome of things.
  - **Truth-Seeking**: Deeply committed to honesty, morality, and seeking the absolute truth.
  - **Generosity**: Large-hearted nature with a willingness to help and guide others.
  - **Visionary**: Capacity for long-range planning and seeing the 'big picture'.

- **Weaknesses**:
  - **Bluntness**: Their commitment to honesty can sometimes transition into tactless or hurtful speech.
  - **Self-Righteousness**: May become overly rigid in their beliefs, assuming they have the moral high ground.
  - **Restlessness**: Intense desire for freedom and movement can make them physically or mentally unstable.
  - **Impatience**: Struggle with details or tasks that require slow, methodical work.
