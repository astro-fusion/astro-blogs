## What is Rahu-Ketu Inclusion and Shadow Planet Influence? {#what-is-title}

**Rahu-Ketu Inclusion and Shadow Planet Influence** represents the recognition and integration of Rahu (the North Lunar Node) and Ketu (the South Lunar Node) as "shadow planets" in the ancient frameworks of Vedic astrology and numerology[1][3][5].
