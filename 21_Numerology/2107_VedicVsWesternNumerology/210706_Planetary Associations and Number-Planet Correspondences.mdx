## What is Planetary Associations and Number-Planet Correspondences? {#what-is-title}

**Planetary Associations and Number-Planet Correspondences** represent the structured system in astrology that links planets with numbers, influencing both personal character and spatial energy. This principle combines:

- **Astrological significance**: Assigning specific planetary rulers to numbers 1-9 and interpreting their influence on human affairs
- **Vastu applications**: Embedding astrological wisdom into the design and energy flow of spaces
- **Spiritual dimensions**: Recognizing the cosmic interplay between numbers, planets, and environmental harmony
