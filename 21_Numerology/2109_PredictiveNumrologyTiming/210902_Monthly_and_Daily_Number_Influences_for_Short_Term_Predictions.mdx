## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Your complete date of birth (day, month, year)
- The current calendar date

**Calculation Formula**:

1. **Personal Year Number**:  
   Add your birth month + birth day + current year. Continue reducing the sum until a single digit emerges.

   \[
   \text{Personal Year} = \text{Birth Month} + \text{Birth Day} + \text{Current Year} \rightarrow \text{Reduce to Single Digit}
   \]
   _Example: April 23, 2025 = 4 (month) + 23 (day) + 2025 (year) = 2052, 2 + 0 + 5 + 2 = 9_[2]

2. **Personal Month Number**:  
   Add your personal year number to the number of the current month (January = 1, February = 2, etc.). Reduce to a single digit.

   \[
   \text{Personal Month} = \text{Personal Year} + \text{Current Month Number} \rightarrow \text{Reduce to Single Digit}
   \]
   _Example: Personal Year 9 + June (6) = 15, 1 + 5 = 6_[2][8]

3. **Personal Day Number**:  
   Add your personal month number to the day's date. Reduce until a single digit remains.

   \[
   \text{Personal Day} = \text{Personal Month} + \text{Day of Month} \rightarrow \text{Reduce to Single Digit}
   \]
   _Example: Personal Month 6 + 27 (June 27) = 33, 3 + 3 = 6_[2]

4. **Interpretation**:  
   Each resulting number (1–9) carries a distinct energy imprint:
   - 1: New beginnings, action, initiative
   - 2: Cooperation, sensitivity, diplomacy
   - 3: Creativity, communication, optimism
   - 4: Organization, hard work, stability
   - 5: Change, freedom, adaptability
   - 6: Responsibility, family, harmony
   - 7: Reflection, analysis, spiritual growth
   - 8: Power, achievement, material success
   - 9: Completion, compassion, letting go[1][5][9]
