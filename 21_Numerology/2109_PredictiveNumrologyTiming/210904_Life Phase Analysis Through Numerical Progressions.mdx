## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Full date of birth: Month, Day, Year

**Calculation Formula**:

1. **Formative Cycle Number**: Reduce your birth month to a single digit (or Master number).  
   - Example: May (5), October (10 → 1+0=1), November (11 stays as 11), December (12 → 1+2=3).

2. **Productive Cycle Number**: Reduce your birth day to a single digit or Master number.  
   - Example: 23 → 2+3=5.

3. **Harvest Cycle Number**: Reduce your birth year to a single digit or Master number.  
   - Example: 1985 → 1+9+8+5=23 → 2+3=5.

4. **Timing**:  
   - Formative Cycle: Birth to late 20s/early 30s (exact age transition varies by Life Path number)  
   - Productive Cycle: Next 27 years  
   - Harvest Cycle: Remainder of life

5. **Interpretation**:  
   - Each number describes the prevailing themes, strengths, and challenges during its cycle.
