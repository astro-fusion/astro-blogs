## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Birth date and full name (for numerology)
- Event name/description and planned calendar date
- Residence coordinates and compass orientation (for Vastu overlay)

**Calculation Formula**:

1. **Life Path Number**: Add all the digits of your birth date until a single digit remains (e.g., 15-05-1990: 1+5+0+5+1+9+9+0 = 30 → 3+0 = 3)[6].
2. **Event Number**: Assign numerical values to each letter in the event name/date, sum, and reduce to a single digit[9].
3. **Personal Year Number**: Add birth month, birth day, and current year, then reduce to a single digit[1].
4. **Compatibility Analysis**: Compare Life Path, Event, and Personal Year numbers. Favorable timing is where synergies exist (e.g., both numbers share harmonious vibrations).
5. **Astrological Overlay**: Identify planetary transits and auspicious yogas that coincide with numerological compatibility for final timing selection[5].
