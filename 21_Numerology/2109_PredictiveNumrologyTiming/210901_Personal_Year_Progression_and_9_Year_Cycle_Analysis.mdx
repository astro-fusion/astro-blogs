## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

To discover your current Personal Year within the 9-Year Cycle:
- **Add your birth month, birth day, and the current year together.**
- Reduce each value to a single digit and then combine them, finally reducing the total to a single digit (1-9).

#### Example Calculation

*Suppose you were born July 15th and want your 2025 Personal Year:*
- July = 7
- Day = 1 + 5 = 6
- 2025 = 2 + 0 + 2 + 5 = 9

Add: 7 + 6 + 9 = 22 → 2 + 2 = 4

So, 2025 is a 4 Personal Year for this individual[5][7][8].
