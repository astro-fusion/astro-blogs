## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Your full date of birth (month, day, year).
- Accurate calculation of your Life Path Number.

**Calculation Formula**:
1. **Life Path Number**: Add together the digits in your month, day, and year of birth to a single digit or master number (11, 22, 33)[6][7].
2. **First Pinnacle**: Add the month and day of birth, reduce to a single digit or master number.
3. **Second Pinnacle**: Add the day and year of birth, reduce.
4. **Third Pinnacle**: Add the First and Second Pinnacle numbers, reduce.
5. **Fourth Pinnacle**: Add the month and year of birth, reduce.

**Timing of Periods**:
- First Pinnacle: From birth to age (36 minus Life Path Number)
- Second Pinnacle: Next 9 years thereafter
- Third Pinnacle: Next 9 years
- Fourth Pinnacle: Remainder of life[1][4][7]

**Example**:  
If born on May 15, 1949 (Month: 5, Day: 6, Year: 5)  
- Life path number: (5 + 6 + 5) = 16, reduced to 7  
- First Pinnacle: 5 (May) + 6 (15) = 11 (no reduction, as master number)
- First Pinnacle ends at age 29 (36 - 7)[1][4].
