## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Birth date and full birth name
- Existing residence layout and spatial orientation

**Calculation Formula**:
- **Life Path Number**: Sum the entire birth date until you reach a single digit or master number
- **Expression/Destiny Number**: Sum the letter values of your full birth name
- **Soul/Heart’s Desire Number**: Sum the vowels in your full name
- **Personality Number**: Sum the consonants in your full name
- **Challenge Numbers**: Calculate the differences in birth date digits
- **Pinnacles & Cycles**: Derive using specific numerological timelines
- **Missing Numbers**: Identify numbers absent from the name (Karmic Lessons)[10]

Apply these values holistically, interrelating their meanings to generate a life narrative mapped to both astrology and Vastu contexts.
