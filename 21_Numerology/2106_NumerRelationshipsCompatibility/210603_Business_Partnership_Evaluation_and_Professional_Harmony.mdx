## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Birth dates and names of all partners or key team members
- Company incorporation date (for business numerology)
- Office layout, orientation, and directional placement data

**Calculation Formula**:

1. **Numerical Compatibility**:  
   - Add individual birth dates (dd + mm + yyyy) to get each person’s Life Path Number.
   - Reduce the sum to a single digit (unless it's a master number: 11, 22, 33).
   - Compare Life Path Numbers among partners/employees for harmonious combinations (e.g., 2 and 8 often work well together, 4 and 5 may clash).

2. **Astrological Compatibility**:  
   - Analyze the 7th house (partnerships), 10th house (career), and 11th house (gains/networks) in each chart.
   - Look for positive planetary aspects (e.g., Venus in the 7th supports harmony).

3. **Vastu Analysis**:  
   - Map each person’s workspace according to their favorable directions (based on their ascendant).
   - Adjust colors, seating, and entryways for optimal energy flow.

**Social Network Analysis (SNA)** can also be integrated to assess the strength and quality of relationships within teams, using graph theory and connectivity metrics[3].
