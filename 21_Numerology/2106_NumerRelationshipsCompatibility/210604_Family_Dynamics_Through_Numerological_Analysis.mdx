## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Full birth dates and names of all family members
- Current house number and layout (for Vastu applications)

**Calculation Formula**:

1. **Life Path Number**:  
   Add all digits of the birth date until reduced to a single digit:
   \[
   \text{YYYY} + \text{MM} + \text{DD} \rightarrow \text{reduce to one digit}
   \]
   Example: 2001-06-22: 2+0+0+1+0+6+2+2 = 13 \rightarrow 1+3=4

2. **Expression/Destiny Number**:  
   Assign numbers to letters of the full name and reduce to a single digit.

3. **House Number Analysis**:  
   Sum the digits of your house or apartment number. Reduce to a single digit for interpretation[7].

4. **Comparative Analysis**:  
   - Analyze compatibility grids (e.g., 1 vs. 6, 2 vs. 8)
   - Look for repeating or missing numbers within the family unit
   - Note generational patterns and recurring themes
