## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Full birth date (for numerology calculations)
- Residence layout and orientation (for Vastu synergy)
- Areas of concern or goals (e.g., financial improvement, relationship harmony)

**Calculation Formula**:

1. **Driver and Conductor Numbers**: 
   - Driver = Day of birth (reduced to a single digit)
   - Conductor = Total of full date of birth (reduced to a single digit)
2. **Challenge Numbers**:
   - Early Challenge: Birth month – Birth day
   - Middle Challenge: Birth day – Birth year
   - Main Challenge: Birth month – Birth year

3. **Compatibility Assessment**:
