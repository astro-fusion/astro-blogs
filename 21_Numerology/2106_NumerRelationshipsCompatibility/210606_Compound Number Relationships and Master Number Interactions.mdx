## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Full birth name and date of birth (for personal charts)
- Full address or room number (for Vastu analysis)
- Concerns, goals, or areas to improve (health, wealth, relationships)

**Calculation Formula**:
1. Convert each letter in the name/address to its numerological value (Chaldean or Pythagorean method).
2. Add all numbers in the date or name for the first total.
3. If the result is a double-digit, check if it is a master number (11, 22, 33) or karmic debt number (13, 14, 16, 19, 44).
4. Analyze both the double-digit (compound/master/karmic) and its reduced single-digit.
5. For addresses or spaces, consider both the unit number and the overall property number.

**Example**:  
Name total = 29 (2 + 9 = 11, master number)  
Address = 14 (karmic number, 1 + 4 = 5, but "14/5" is significant in its own right)[2][5].

## Frequently Asked Questions {#faqs}

**Q: What are the “master numbers” in numerology?**  
A: The master numbers are 11, 22, and 33—double-digit numbers with intensified spiritual and creative power that do not get reduced in numerological calculations[1][3][6].

**Q: What are “karmic debt numbers” and why are they important?**  
A: Karmic debt numbers (13, 14, 16, 19, 44) signify unresolved lessons and repeating life patterns. Their presence in charts, names, or addresses indicates areas requiring conscious effort and transformation[5].

**Q: How do compound numbers influence relationship compatibility?**  
A: Some compound numbers blend well, while others create energetic challenges. Compatibility analysis involves examining the interplay of personal and environmental numbers to identify harmony or friction points[2][4].

**Q: Should I change my house or business number if it’s a difficult compound number?**
