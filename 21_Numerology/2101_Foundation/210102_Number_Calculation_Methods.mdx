## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Full birth date (day, month, year)
- Complete name (as per birth certificate or legal documents)

**Calculation Formula**:

**1. Reducing Multi-Digit Numbers to Single Digits**
   - For dates: Add each digit (day + month + year) together.
   - Continue adding the result's digits until you reach a single digit, unless the result is 11, 22, or 33 (Master Numbers)[1][6][10].

**Example**:
- Birth date: 15 August 1990
- 1 + 5 + 8 + 1 + 9 + 9 + 0 = 33  
  Since 33 is a master number, stop reducing.

**2. Understanding When to Stop Reduction for Master Numbers**
- Master Numbers (11, 22, 33) carry unique vibrations and are not reduced further in core calculations like Life Path or Expression numbers. In cycles or subsidiary calculations, however, master numbers are sometimes reduced[6][10].

**3. Converting Names into Numerical Values**
- Assign numeric values to each letter using a chosen numerology table.
- Sum the values for the entire name.
- Reduce to a single digit or acknowledge if the sum is a master number[4][7].

**4. Mathematical Foundation in Vedic Numerology**
- Vedic numerology aligns each number with a planetary energy:  
  1 - Sun  
  2 - Moon  
  3 - Jupiter  
  4 - Rahu  
  5 - Mercury  
  6 - Venus  
  7 - Ketu  
  8 - Saturn  
  9 - Mars  
- This foundational mapping guides both interpretation and practical applications in astrology and Vastu[1][4][7].
