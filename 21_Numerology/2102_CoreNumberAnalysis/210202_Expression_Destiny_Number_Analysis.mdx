## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Your full legal birth name as found on your birth certificate (first, middle, and last name)

**Calculation Formula**:

1. **Write Your Full Birth Name**: Include every given name.
2. **Assign Numbers to Each Letter**: Use the standard Pythagorean numerology chart[6][8]:

   | Letter     | Number |
   |------------|--------|
   | A, J, S    | 1      |
   | B, K, T    | 2      |
   | C, L, U    | 3      |
   | D, M, V    | 4      |
   | E, N, W    | 5      |
   | F, O, X    | 6      |
   | G, P, Y    | 7      |
   | H, Q, Z    | 8      |
   | I, R       | 9      |

3. **Convert Each Name Separately**: Add the values for each name (first, middle, last) and reduce each to a single digit or Master Number (11, 22, 33).
4. **Add the Single Digits/Master Numbers**: Combine the three results and reduce to a single digit or Master Number if needed.

**Example**:  
For "Jane Marie Doe":

- Jane: J(1) + A(1) + N(5) + E(5) = 12 → 1 + 2 = 3
- Marie: M(4) + A(1) + R(9) + I(9) + E(5) = 28 → 2 + 8 = 10 → 1 + 0 = 1
- Doe: D(4) + O(6) + E(5) = 15 → 1 + 5 = 6
- Final Expression Number: 3 + 1 + 6 = 10 → 1 + 0 = 1[3][6][8]

**Interpretation**:  
- If you arrive at 11, 22, or 33, these are powerful Master Numbers and are not further reduced.
- Your Expression_Destiny Number reveals your core talents, mission, and the unique path you are destined to walk in life.

**Q1. Can I use my preferred or married name for Expression_Destiny Number Analysis?**

**Q2. What are Master Numbers, and why are they special?**

**Q3. How is the Destiny Number different from the Life Path Number?**
