## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Your full birth name, exactly as it appears on your birth certificate.

**Calculation Formula**:

1. **Write down your complete birth name.**
   - Example: JAMIE RAE WILKINSON

2. **Isolate all consonants in your name.**
   - JAMIE: J, M
   - RAE: R
   - WILKINSON: W, L, K, N, S, N

3. **Assign each consonant its corresponding numerological value using the Pythagorean chart:**

   | Number | Letters          |
   |--------|-----------------|
   | 1      | J, S            |
   | 2      | B, K, T         |
   | 3      | C, L            |
   | 4      | D, M, V         |
   | 5      | N, W            |
   | 6      | F, X            |
   | 7      | G, P, Y         |
   | 8      | H, Q, Z         |
   | 9      | R               |

   - JAMIE: J (1), M (4)
   - RAE: R (9)
   - WILKINSON: W (5), L (3), K (2), N (5), S (1), N (5)

4. **Add the numbers together:**
   - 1 + 4 + 9 + 5 + 3 + 2 + 5 + 1 + 5 = 35

5. **Reduce the sum to a single digit (unless you get master numbers 11, 22, or 33):**
   - 3 + 5 = 8

**Result:** Jamie Rae Wilkinson’s Personality Number is **8**[3][5][6].
