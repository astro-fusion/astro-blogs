## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Life Path Number Calculation

**Required Information**:
- Your complete date of birth (day, month, year)

**Calculation Formula**:
1. **Reduce each part of your birth date to a single digit or Master Number (11, 22, 33):**
   - Month: Add digits (e.g., October = 1 + 0 = 1)
   - Day: Add digits (e.g., 22 = Master Number, do not reduce)
   - Year: Add digits (e.g., 1984 = 1 + 9 + 8 + 4 = 22 — a Master Number — don’t reduce)
2. **Add the three results:**
   - Example: 1 (month) + 22 (day) + 22 (year) = 45
3. **Reduce the sum to a single digit or Master Number:**
   - 4 + 5 = 9 (Life Path Number is 9)
   - Retain Master Numbers 11, 22, or 33 if they appear.

**Example Calculation:**
- **April 14, 1992**
  - Month: 4
  - Day: 1 + 4 = 5
  - Year: 1 + 9 + 9 + 2 = 21; 2 + 1 = 3
  - Total: 4 + 5 + 3 = 12; 1 + 2 = 3
  - **Life Path Number = 3**[1][3][5].

## Common Mistakes to Avoid {#common-mistakes}

- **Incorrect Calculation**: Do not add all birth date digits in a row; always reduce month, day, and year separately before summing[3][4].
- **Ignoring Master Numbers**: Never reduce 11, 22, or 33 to single digits during the process; retain their master vibration[1][4].
- **Confusing with Name Numbers**: Life Path is based strictly on birth date—not your name or official documents.
- **Overlooking Environmental Context**: Failing to adapt Vastu remedies to your geographic and cultural setting can dampen effects.
