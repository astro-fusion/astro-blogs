- **Astrological significance**: The Maturity number emerges from combining your Life Path and Expression (Destiny) numbers. It indicates your “true self” as you mature, describing the qualities, goals, and desires that come to fruition in the second half of life[1][3][5].

## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Your full date of birth (for Life Path number)
- The full name at birth (for Expression/Destiny number)

**Calculation Formula**:

1. **Calculate the Life Path Number**:
   - Add all digits in your birthdate, then reduce to a single digit or master number[8].
   - Example: August 12, 1984 → 8 + 1 + 2 + 1 + 9 + 8 + 4 = 33 (master number, do not reduce further)[8].

2. **Calculate the Expression (Destiny) Number**:
   - Assign each letter of your birth name a number (A=1, B=2...I=9, then continue), add each component, and reduce as above[10].

3. **Combine the Two**:
   - Add the Life Path and Expression numbers. If the result is double digits other than 11, 22, or 33, reduce it to a single digit[1][4][5]:
   - Example: Life Path 1 + Expression 4 = 5 (1+4=5)[1].

#### Quick Example

- Life Path: 7
- Expression: 3  
7 + 3 = 10 → 1 + 0 = 1  
**Maturity Number is 1**

**Master Numbers Exception**: If the sum is 11, 22, or 33, do not reduce further.

**Interpretation**: 
- Each Maturity number has specific meanings (see next section for advanced interpretation)[1][2][7].
