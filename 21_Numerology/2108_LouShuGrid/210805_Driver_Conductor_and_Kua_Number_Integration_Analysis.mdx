## What is Driver, Conductor and Kua Number Integration Analysis? {#what-is-title}

**Driver, Conductor and Kua Number Integration Analysis** is an advanced methodology in numerology that fuses three major personal numbers—Driver (Moolank), Conductor (Bhagyank or Life Path), and Kua—into the Lou Shu Grid system. This process reveals a multidimensional portrait of a person's inherent strengths, life path, and environmental harmony.

- **Driver Number (Moolank)**: Extracted from your birth date, representing your core identity, instincts, and natural tendencies[1][2][8].
- **Conductor Number (Bhagyank)**: Calculated from your complete birth date and reflecting your life journey, lessons, and spiritual evolution[1][2][8].
- **Kua Number**: Derived from your birth year (with different calculations for male and female), signifying energetic compatibility, auspicious directions, and potential for environmental resonance[2][3][9].

### Historical Context

The integration of these three numbers has its roots in both Vedic and Chinese metaphysical traditions. Ancient texts like the Brihat Samhita, as well as the Lou Shu (Lo Shu) Square in Chinese numerology, document the deep links between personal numbers and the energy blueprint of one's living or working space[2][9].

## Calculation and Analysis Methods {#calculation}

### Step-by-Step Calculation Process

**Required Information**:
- Your complete date of birth (DD/MM/YYYY)
- Gender (for Kua calculation)
- The orientation of your current residence or workspace

**Calculation Formula**:

- **Driver Number**: Add the day of your birth until it’s a single digit (e.g., 23 = 2+3 = 5)[1][2][8].
- **Conductor Number (Life Path)**: Sum your entire date of birth until a single digit (e.g., 23/07/1990 = 2+3+0+7+1+9+9+0 = 31 = 3+1 = 4)[1][2][8].
- **Kua Number**:
    - For males: Subtract the single digit sum of your birth year from 11 (e.g., 1990: 1+9+9+0=19=1+9=10=1+0=1; 11-1=10=1+0=1)[2][3][6].
    - For females: Add 4 to the single digit sum of your birth year (if result is more than 9, reduce again to a single digit)[2][3].

**Analysis**:
- Enter these numbers into your Lou Shu Grid.
- Assign weightage: Driver (100%), Conductor (75-80%), Kua (25%).
- Identify missing or dominant numbers, and interpret their implications for your mental, emotional, and material well-being[2][9].
