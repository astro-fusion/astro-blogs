## Introduction

In [Vedic astrology](https://www.astrology.com/vedic), the conjunction of **Sun** and **Jupiter** creates one of the most revered and beneficial planetary combinations known as **"Guru Aditya Yoga"**. This majestic alignment merges the king (Sun) with the guru (Jupiter), producing individuals blessed with wisdom, prosperity, moral authority, and divine grace.

<Card>
<CardContent className="p-4">
**Ancient Wisdom**: Classical texts like Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra describe this combination as "Raja Yoga Karaka" - the creator of royal fortune. It's said that even a poorly placed conjunction can elevate a person's status through wisdom and righteous action.
</CardContent>
</Card>

Unlike the fiery intensity of [Sun and Mars conjunction](/conjunctions/sun-mars-conjunction) or the intellectual focus of [Sun and Mercury conjunction](/conjunctions/sun-mercury-conjunction), this alignment brings expansive wisdom, moral leadership, and divine blessings that create lasting positive impact.

## Understanding Guru Aditya Yoga

**Guru Aditya Yoga** forms when Jupiter (Guru) and Sun (Aditya) occupy the same zodiac sign, creating a powerful synergy between divine wisdom and royal authority. This combination is considered the "Midas Touch" of Vedic astrology.

### Formation Conditions

- **Primary**: Sun and Jupiter in same sign
- **Secondary**: Within 6-10 degrees of each other for maximum effect
- **Enhanced**: When conjunction occurs in Jupiter's own signs (Sagittarius, Pisces)
- **Royal**: When formed in Sun's sign (Leo) - called "Simha Guru Yoga"

### Mythological Significance

In Vedic mythology, this conjunction represents:
- **Brihaspati (Jupiter) blessing Surya (Sun)** with divine wisdom
- **The ideal relationship between King and Guru**
- **Dharma (righteousness) governing Artha (wealth)**
- **Material success blessed by spiritual wisdom**
