In [Vedic astrology](https://www.astrology.com/article/vedic-astrology-introduction/), the **Sun and Moon conjunction**, also known as **Amavasya** or the **New Moon** phase, represents one of the most significant planetary alignments in a birth chart. This powerful celestial event occurs when both luminaries occupy the same zodiac sign and degree, creating a unique fusion of conscious (Sun) and subconscious (Moon) energies.

## Understanding Planetary Conjunctions

A **planetary conjunction** in Vedic astrology occurs when two or more planets occupy the same zodiac sign or are within 8-10 degrees of each other in a birth chart. The Sun-Moon conjunction is particularly special because it involves the two most important luminaries in astrology.

### What Makes Sun-Moon Conjunction Unique?

- **Luminaries Union**: Combines the two primary lights of the zodiac
- **Conscious-Subconscious Merger**: Aligns rational mind with emotional nature
- **New Moon Energy**: Carries the power of new beginnings and fresh starts
- **Karmic Significance**: Often indicates important soul lessons related to self-identity
