The 8th house, known as **Randhra Bhava** or **Ayu Bhava** in Sanskrit, governs longevity, transformation, occult sciences, shared wealth, inheritance, sudden events, and regeneration. Mars here brings its dynamic energy to these profound and often hidden aspects of life.

2. **Occult and Mystical Interests:** Natural attraction to esoteric knowledge and hidden sciences
3. **Shared Resources:** Complex dynamics with joint finances, inheritance, and others' money
4. **Longevity Factors:** Impact on health, accidents, and life span considerations
