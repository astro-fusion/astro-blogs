## Understanding Rahu in 7th House

### The 7th House: Foundation of Partnerships and Relationships

The **7th House** in astrology governs:
- **Marriage and spousal relationships** - life partner, marital harmony, marriage timing
- **Business partnerships** - collaborations, joint ventures, business alliances
- **Legal matters** - contracts, agreements, court cases, litigation
- **Public relations** - reputation, public image, social interactions
- **Open enemies** - known opponents, competitors, conflicts
- **Foreign travel and connections** - international business, overseas relationships
- **Trade and commerce** - buying, selling, negotiations, deals

When [Rahu's transformative energy](https://astrofusion.com/rahu-north-node-vedic-astrology) influences this house, it brings unconventional approaches to all these life areas while creating both extraordinary opportunities and unique challenges in relationships and partnerships.
