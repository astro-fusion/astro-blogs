**Characteristics of the Moon**

The Moon is a watery planet. It is cold, moist, and feminine. It
represents the mind, the mother, and the emotions. It is also
the significator of beauty, charm, and happiness. The Moon is
exalted in Taurus and debilitated in Scorpio. It is friendly to
the Sun, Mercury, and Jupiter, and inimical to Mars and
Saturn. Venus is neutral to the Moon.

The Moon's gem is the pearl. Its metal is silver. Its direction is
northwest. Its day is Monday. Its color is white. Its element
is water. Its taste is salty. Its quality is rajasic. Its deity is
Chandra.

The Moon rules the sign Cancer. It is the lord of the fourth
house in the natural zodiac.

The Moon is a benefic planet. It gives good results when it is
well-placed in a horoscope. However, it can also give bad
results when it is ill-placed.

The Moon is a very important planet in astrology. It is the
